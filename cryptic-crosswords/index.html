Cryptic crossword clues

There's a game called Minute Cryptic and it's really fun. It's based on a thing called Cryptic crosswords, which are like crosswords but the clues are really cryptic. There are rules to how they work that are pretty difficult to learn, but basically there's a definition somewhere in the clue that's a synonym ish of the answer, and some wordplay you have to do to get the answer. There's a tutorial that's more in depth. Minute Cryptic is just a NYT-style daily game where they make one Cryptic crossword clue per day. I've been playing it for a while, and now I've also made some clues of my own.

These are placed in order of creation. Perhaps that means they're also in ascending order of quality, but that's not for me to say...

Click the links to get clues. Also no, I will not give you the answers.
