Cryptic crossword clues

There's a game called Minute Cryptic and it's really fun. It's based on a thing called Cryptic crosswords, which are like crosswords but the clues are really cryptic. There are rules to how they work that are pretty complicated, but basically there's a definition somewhere in the clue that's a synonym ish of the answer, and some wordplay you have to do to get the answer. And the number at the end is the number of letters in the answer. There's a tutorial that's more in depth. Minute Cryptic is just a NYT-style daily game where they make one Cryptic crossword clue per day. I've been playing it for a while, and now I've also made some clues of my own.

Click the links to get clues, and check your guesses with the text boxes!
