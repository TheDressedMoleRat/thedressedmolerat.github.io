Dressed Mole Rat's custom ambigrams

My generator sometimes makes something really nice, but no generator can really compete with human made ambigrams. That's why I offer custom ambigrams drawn by me for just $30 an ambigram! Email me at soweliawa@gmail.com with what word or words you want, and what style you would like as well. If you want, I can provide a sketch for free, and then charge to develop a finished and vectorized ambigram with your feedback! If you want to pay me from the start, you can do so immediately on this Stripe Payment Page. After that, it can take between a few days and a few weeks before you're happy with the design, and you can receive it in whatever format you'd like.
